    Get a glimpse on how Android Wear works

    Follow the instructions below to explore.

    • First, you’ll need to wake up your watch. Touch anywhere on the watch’s screen to wake it up. You can also turn your wrist towards you, or press a button if your watch has one.

    • To see a card, swipe up from the bottom of your screen. Too see more cards, keep swiping from the bottom of the screen.

    • Some cards provide more details or have actions you can take. Swipe from right to left you can take different action. With phone, you can press call later or open on your phone for further action. The actions will vary depending on the card.

    • Once you’re done looking at a card, you can remove it. Swipe from left to right to get rid of a card. The next card on your watch will appear. Cards will automatically come back when there’s new info to share. If you remove a card on your watch, the same card or notification will be dismissed on your phone.
